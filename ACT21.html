Pokemon

Pikachu

Pikachu can generate electricity through its cheeks, unleashing powerful shocks to defend itself and its friends. It’s well-known as the mascot of the Pokémon franchise and has a playful, friendly nature.

Charizard

Charizard can unleash fiery breath that is hot enough to melt boulders. This dragon-like Pokémon is known for its fierce loyalty and is a popular choice among trainers for its powerful fire-based attacks.
